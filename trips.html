Our Trips

Options for families, beginners, and experienced rafters. Below are details and what’s included.

Contact Us About Trips

River Rush Adventure

Difficulty: Intermediate (Class III–IV). Duration: ~3 hours. Includes: PFD, helmet, certified guide, photos.

Great for paddlers seeking excitement with a mix of technical rapids and scenic sections. Recommended minimum age: 12.

Family Float Trip

Difficulty: Easy (Class I–II). Duration: ~2 hours. Includes: PFD, guide.

A calm, scenic float perfect for families and first-timers. Plenty of photo ops and wildlife viewing. Recommended minimum age: 6.

Extreme Rapids Expedition

Difficulty: Advanced (Class IV–V). Duration: ~5 hours. Includes: Guide, safety briefing (participants must be experienced).

For experienced rafters who want a full adrenaline experience. Safety briefing and proper equipment required. Recommended minimum age: 16.
